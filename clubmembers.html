Captains are decided based on the club's seniority policy, the longest serving members.

As of 31 August 2021

No. Pos. Nation Player Captain
12 DF BRA Marcelo Captain

Besides the seniority policy the captain system works as follows. If all the players are on the field at the same time, Marcelo is the captain. If he is not on the field then it is Benzema who wears the captain's armband; and so on and so forth.
